# Mercury Computers Limited — Company Profile

*Source: [mercurycomputerslimited.com](https://mercurycomputerslimited.com/). Summaries below were rephrased for compliance with content licensing.*

## At a Glance

Mercury Computers Limited is one of Uganda's leading independent retailers of ICT
technology and office supplies, based in Kampala. The company sells computer
hardware and electronics from major global brands and also operates a technical
services arm covering repairs, IT support, networking, and CCTV. It has been
trading for roughly 17+ years.

## Who They Are

Mercury Computers positions itself as a trusted, independent retailer stocking
hundreds of products, laptops, desktops, tablets and more, from big brands at
competitive prices. Beyond sales, they emphasize personal service and long-term
client relationships, backed by an in-house team of technicians. Their pitch is
that almost every modern business depends on IT (computers, printers, CCTV,
routers, switches, cabling), and Mercury covers both the hardware supply and the
expertise to support it.

Headline trust signals used on the site: 20+ years experience, 1000+ happy
clients, 50+ brand partners, and 24/7 support.

## Brands & Partnerships

- **Microsoft Authorized Partner** and Cloud Solution Provider (CSP). Supplies
  genuine Microsoft 365 (Business Standard & Premium), Windows 11 Pro (OEM and
  Volume), and Windows Server 2025. Services include deployment, email migration,
  security hardening (Defender, Intune, Entra ID), and end-user training for
  businesses, banks, NGOs, schools, and government departments.
- **Key brand partners:** HP, Dell, Lenovo, Microsoft, Cisco, Fortinet (plus
  Apple and Epson products sold).

## Services (Beyond Retail)

- Repairs for laptops, desktops, and printers (Mac and Windows friendly)
- Same/next-day turnaround on the majority of issues; onsite or in-shop
- Free callouts, customers only pay for the work performed
- Weekend and evening callouts with local technicians
- Full business IT support: maintenance, networking, security, and hardware advice
- CCTV, routers, switches, cabling, and web-related services
